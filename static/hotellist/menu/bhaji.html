Bhaji

Ingredients

2 medium Potatoes (approx. 1½ cups chopped)

1/2 cup Green Peas (fresh or frozen)

3/4 cup chopped Cauliflower

1/2 cup chopped Carrot

1 large Onion, chopped

1 tablespoon Ginger Garlic Paste

1½ teaspoons Red Chilli Powder

1 teaspoon Readymade Pav Bhaji Masala Powder

1½ teaspoons Red Chilli Powder

1/4 teaspoon Turmeric Powder

2 tablespoons finely chopped Coriander Leaves

Butter for serving

Directions

  1. Pressure cook the cauliflower and potato until completely tender (3 whistles)
  2. Mash well with the water used to cook it. Set aside ,Heat the oil and add chopped capsicum. Cook for 2-3 mins until it begins to soften
  3. Add the ginger and garlic. Saute for a few seconds Add chilli powder and turmeric powder. Fry for 5 seconds and add the mashed vegetables Mix well and add tomato puree along with 1 cup water
  4. Bring to boil and add pav bhaji masala and peas Cook covered for 10-15 mins Open and cook further until the desired consistency is reached Add salt and butter. Mix well, and remove from fire
  5. When ready to serve, butter the pav generously and toast until golden on a griddle or tawa Add chopped onions and coriander leaves on the bhaji. Squeeze lime juice and eat immediately
